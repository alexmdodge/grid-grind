Your Goal

So I heard you like puzzles! How about one that never ends? In Grid Grind your goal is to face off against yourself and others, using nothing but your wit. How far can you get?

How to Play

The game is simple, click the blocks to change their color. Match up at least three in a group and you’ll score points. No diagonals!

The Catch

The more you match up in a group, the more points you’ll get! The trick is, the blocks change color according to the pattern you see on the side. Run out of lives and you start over.
